FAQ

Common questions.

How fast is delivery?
Standard delivery is 2–4 business days for short-form. Long-form takes 4–7 days. Add the Fast Delivery add-on to get your edit within 24–48 hours.
How many revisions are included?
Each package includes 1 revision round by default. Extra rounds can be added at checkout or after project creation. The Scale retainer plan includes unlimited revisions.
Can I send Google Drive links?
Yes — the creative brief form accepts Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, and Frame.io links for all asset types including raw footage, brand guides, and reference videos.
Do you provide source files?
Source files (Premiere Pro or DaVinci project) can be added as an optional add-on. By default, you receive an MP4 export and optionally SRT subtitle files.
What happens after payment?
You are redirected to the Creative Brief Form. Once the brief and assets are submitted, your project moves into the production queue. You can track status live from your client dashboard.
