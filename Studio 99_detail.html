FAQ

Questions about this service.

What format should I submit my raw footage in?
We accept MP4, MOV, MXF, and most standard video formats. You can share via Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or Frame.io. Please ensure shared folders have the correct permissions.
Does production start immediately after payment?
Production starts only after your creative brief and raw assets are fully submitted. This ensures the editor has everything they need to deliver without back-and-forth delays.
What counts as a revision?
One revision round includes all your feedback notes submitted at one time via the revision form. We encourage you to compile all notes before submitting to make the most of your included round.
Can I switch packages after ordering?
Package upgrades are possible before production starts. Contact support from your dashboard and we'll adjust your order accordingly. Downgrades are not available after payment.
How do I track my project?
After checkout, you'll have access to your client dashboard where you can see real-time project status, submit assets, view drafts, and request revisions — all in one place.
